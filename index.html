Crystals Collector!

At the start of this game a random number will be given to you.

By clicking on one of the four crystals below you will add a certain amount of points your score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each crystal has a hidden value and will only show their value once you click on the crystal.

Everytime the game restarts the values of each crystal will change.
